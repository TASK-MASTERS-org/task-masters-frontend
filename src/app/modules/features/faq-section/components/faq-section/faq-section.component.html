Explore the useful FAQs

1. What is Task-Masters? Task-Masters is a web-based labor management system that connects customers with skilled laborers for various service needs. 2. Who can join Task-Masters? Both customers and laborers can register with Task-Masters. 3. What are the benefits of using Task-Masters? Customers: Find reliable laborers at competitive prices, read reviews, and track order deliveries. Laborers: Secure jobs based on expertise, get rated by customers, and enjoy a wider client base. 4. How do I register for Task-Masters? Simply visit our website and register as a customer. We also offer convenient Google authentication for faster registration. 5. How can I post a job request? Once registered, you can create a job post specifying your service needs and desired budget. 6. How does the bidding process work? Laborers will review your job post and submit bids with their proposed prices. You can choose the laborer that best suits your needs and budget. 7. Can I see reviews for laborers? Yes, the system allows you to view feedback and ratings left by previous customers for each laborer. 8. How do I track my order after making a purchase from the store? Our system provides a dedicated order tracking feature. You can easily monitor the status of your delivery and manage any related inquiries with the administrator. 9. What payment methods do you accept? Task-Masters offers a variety of secure payment options, including credit/debit cards, digital wallets, and other popular online platforms. 10. How does the payment section work? The payment section facilitates secure transactions between customers, laborers, and the platform. You can choose your preferred payment method during checkout.
